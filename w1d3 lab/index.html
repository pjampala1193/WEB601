About Us

Happy Paws Pet Adoption is a local non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming pets in our community. We work closely with volunteers and veterinarians to ensure all animals are healthy and ready for adoption.

Why Adopt From Us?

  • All pets are vaccinated
  • Affordable adoption fees
  • Friendly and trained staff
  • Lifetime support for adopters
