About Splashes

Our Philosophy

Our mission is to educate and motivate individuals to be safer and learn proper swimming
technique.

We have chosen to share our love of swimming with children and believe there is a swimmer in
every child. We pride ourselves on teaching the necessary skills needed to become a
comfortable, proficient swimmer right from the beginning.

We Do A Pretty Good Job

Experience excellence through Splashes’ award-winning programs where
safety, fast progress, and a fun environment come first. Join the swim school
that loves what they do and it shows.
