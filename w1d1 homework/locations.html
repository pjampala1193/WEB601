Cliffs Locations

Explore four famous cliff destinations in California. Each location offers unique views and activities.

West Cliff

The most popular and most visited cliff. Great for sunsets and ocean views.

North Cliff

Cooler weather and great hiking. Perfect for scenic walks and photography.

East Cliff

Known for mountain views and canyon vibes. A quieter and peaceful spot.

South Cliff

Great for camping and outdoor fun. A popular destination for groups.
