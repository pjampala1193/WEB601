Our Cliff Locations

Great Cliffs features four unique cliff locations, each offering different landscapes, views, and recreational opportunities.

Cliff Descriptions

  • West Cliff: Popular for college hikes and scenic sunsets.
  • North Cliff: Family-friendly area with camping options.
  • East Cliff: Known for adventure trails and youth activities.
  • South Cliff: Ideal for extreme sports and overnight camping.
